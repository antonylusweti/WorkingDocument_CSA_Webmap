Synopsis

The Climate Smart Agriculture Youth Network (CSAYN) aims to engage youth through information dissemination by using existing knowledge materials on CSA

Goals

To increase the capacities of youth members on CSA through social media and other information outlets

Objectives

(a) Raise awareness CSA among young men and women (aged 18-35) to enable them to make sustainable decisions for the future in the agriculture sector' b) Create awareness of present and future threats related to climate change and agriculture; c) Make youths aware of the contributions they can make in the agriculture sector for a better future, especially through the application of CSA practices in both agriculture and forestry; d) Enhance meaningful youth contribution in livestock, fisheries and aquaculture activities.

CSA Activities and interventions

All CSA interventions: Agroforestry; Climate Smart Crops; Climate smart livestock practices; Livestock feed management; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Policy and Advocacy; Postharvest management; Renewable energy for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

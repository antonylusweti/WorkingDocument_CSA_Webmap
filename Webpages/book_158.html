Synopsis

The Inter-sectoral Forum on Agro-biodiversity and Agro-ecology (ISFAA) to promote mainstreaming of Biodiversity and ecosystem service within the agriculture sector and its landscapes.

Goals

To provide a platform through which stakeholders on agro biodiversity and agro ecology can interact to share Knowledge and information, influence policy and fundraise.

Objectives

a) To inform members about organic approaches to agriculture in Kenya; b) provide space for exchanging experiences and opinions; c) develop concrete measures in small groups, such as recommendations for action, political statements or university teaching programmes

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

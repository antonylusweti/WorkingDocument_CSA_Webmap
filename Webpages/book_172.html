Synopsis

The Tanzania Climate Smart Agriculture Alliance (TCSAA) is a platform for the promotion of Climate Smart Agriculture (CSA) across United Republic of Tanzania (Mainland and Zanzibar), with the aim of creating a truly national and broad-based forum on CSA in Tanzania through effective linkages with key CSA initiatives at the Region, Africa and Global levels.

Goals

Creating a truly national and broad-based forum on CSA in Tanzania through effective linkages with key CSA initiatives at the Region, Africa and Global levels.

Objectives

To serve as a multi-stakeholder platform for the consolidated coordination of actors involved in CSA in order to ensure coherence in the implementation of CSA initiatives, learning and experience sharing. Hence, promote and accelerate widespread adoption of CSA approaches, technologies and best practices in a coordinated manner by strengthening and where possible, create partnerships and synergies in CSA approaches across Tanzania.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

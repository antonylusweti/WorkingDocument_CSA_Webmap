Synopsis

The purpose of the SCIPEA project was to strengthen climate partnerships and facilitated the co- development of tailored services with climate information providers and users.

Goals

The goal is to work with national meteorological and hydrological services (NMHSs), universities and training centres in the region to strengthen training resources and the capacity for climate service development.

Objectives

a) Strengthening engagement between climate information providers and users leading to the co-design of prototype climate services; b) Improved links and data flows between Global Producing Centres, ICPAC and NMHSs to strengthen the quality of climate information services

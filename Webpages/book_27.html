Synopsis

The programme is designed to reduce the impact of climate change, enhance economic growth and reduce rural poverty.

Goals

The programme aims to improve farmers' prosperity, food security and nutrition through scaling up best practices

Objectives

a) To improve the access of farmers to a secure irrigation production system and enhance water efficiency through climate-smart agriculture in the adjacent watersheds; b) support linkages to markets and services so that smallholder farmers can increase their productivity, competitiveness and incomes; c) enhance their resilience against external shocks and those induced by adverse weather and climate conditions, such as drought.

CSA Activities and interventions

Smart water management; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Crop Disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Weather & Agroadvisory Services

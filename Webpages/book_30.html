Synopsis

The project highlights the importance of farmers’ local knowledge and the opportunities for integrating it with global knowledge to improve the performance of agricultural systems in Ethiopia.

Goals

To support the continued development of a knowledge base documenting the state of intra- and inter-specific food legume diversity and of farmers’ indigenous botanical and agricultural knowledge farmers about their landraces’ food uses, their management and ecological and socio­economic roles in local farming systems in Ethiopia

Objectives

a) to capacity build on researchers; b) Improved advocacy for smallholder agricultural systems via policies that encourage broad rather than narrow use of genetic resources.

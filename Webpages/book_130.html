Synopsis

The project geared towards strengthening the resilience of agropastoral communities and the local government in Karamoja, to reduce the impacts of climate risks on livelihoods in the region.

Goals

To strengthen the resilience of local rural communities to climate risks and other shocks.

Objectives

a) Early warning, preparedness and contingency planning and response system; b) Livestock disease surveillance, diagnostic capacity, veterinary services and animal/ livestock nutrition; c) Aro-pastoral production systems through support to district local governments, APFS and improved access to water

CSA Activities and interventions

Smart water management; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Weather & Agroadvisory Services

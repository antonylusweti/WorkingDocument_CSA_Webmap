Synopsis

Third Phase of Transformation of Agriculture Sector Program Project for Rwanda is to increase and intensify the productivity of the Rwandan agricultural and livestock sectors and expand the development of value chains.

Goals

program supports the Government of Rwanda’s strategic objectives of the Transformation of Agriculture Sector Program Phase 3 with aims to enhance food security and nutrition contributing to a reduction in poverty and inclusive economic growth.

Objectives

a) Agriculture and animal resource intensification; b) Research, technology transfer and organization of farmers; c) Private sector-driven value chain development and expanded investments; d) Institutional results-focused development and agricultural cross-cutting issues.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Markets,credit\finance and value chains for CSA.

Synopsis

Programme contribute towards achieving Ethiopia's ‘Climate Resilient Green Economy’ (CRGE) strategy through capacity building and promotion of sustainable land management.

Goals

Programme's goals is to rehabilitate degraded watersheds, develop the potential of rehabilitated watersheds and foster access to carbon finance opportunities from agriculture and participatory forest management actions.

Objectives

Increase the awareness and capacity of targeted government institutions, both at federal and regional levels, and the rural population at large, to deal with climate change.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Agrofoforestry; Soil management

Synopsis

The K-RAPID project will facilitate a process that supports the growth and capacity strengthening of public, civil society, community, and private sector institutions to increase access to water for people and livestock, to put in place a healthy rangeland ecosystem, and to ensure that access to water is both sufficient for these multiple uses, and sustainable over time

Goals

To strengthening public and private institutions in the water and livestock sectors, putting policy frameworks in place to ensure efficient county-wide and cross-border water and rangeland management systems, and to increase access to water and pasture.

Objectives

a) increasing the food security and resilience of dryland communities, b) improving the health of local populations, c) strengthening governance structures at the local, sub-county and county levels

CSA Activities and interventions

capacity building; soil management; climate smart livestock practices/livestock feed management/improved breeds

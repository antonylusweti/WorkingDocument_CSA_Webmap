Synopsis

Program aims at bolstering farmers’ adaptive capacity and support the national vision of achieving food security

Goals

Improving and sustaining agricultural advisory Services – Agro-advisory services that include climate applications for agriculture will help farmers to make better and informed decisions in the face of risks and uncertainties, in addition to the integrated management of present and emerging pests and disease challenges

Objectives

a) To increase use of climate-smart agricultural practices; B) promoting improved land management; c) sustainable crop-livestock and fisheries intensification

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate-smart agricultural practices; improved land management; sustainable crop-livestock and fisheries intensification

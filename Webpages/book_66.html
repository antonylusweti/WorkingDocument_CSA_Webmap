Synopsis

KCEP-CRAL aims to reduce rural poverty and food insecurity among smallholders in Kenya's arid and semi-arid lands by developing their economic potential, while improving their natural resource management capacity and resilience to climate change in an increasingly fragile ecosystem.

Goals

KCEP-CRAL aims to reduce rural poverty and food insecurity among smallholders in Kenya's arid and semi-arid lands by developing their economic potential, while improving their natural resource management capacity and resilience to climate change in an increasingly fragile ecosystem.

Objectives

(i) graduation of smallholder farmers to commercially oriented, climate-resilient agricultural practices through improvements in productivity, post-production management practices and market linkages for targeted value chains. (ii) Empowerment of county governments and communities to sustainable and consensually manage their natural resources and build resilience to climate change.

CSA Activities and interventions

postharvest management; markets,credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Climate smart crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops

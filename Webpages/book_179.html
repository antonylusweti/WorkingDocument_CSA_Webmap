Synopsis

The overall programme goal is to increase availability climate smart food for the growing population in Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda. The emphasis of programme implementation efforts will be on (1) increasing the adoption of climate smart practices and technologies among farmers and agro- enterprises; (2) increasing investments and business growth in climate smart value chains, and (3) creating the enabling environment necessary to ensure large-scale roll out of market driven climate smart agriculture.

Goals

To increase the availability of accessible climate smart food for the growing population in Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda.

Objectives

To transform, reorient and climate-proof agricultural systems and businesses to the new realities of climate change in a select number of value chains.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA

Synopsis

HIGHWAY project looks to enhance the resilience of African people and economic development to weather and climate related shocks, with an inital focus on the Lake Victoria Basin (LVB).

Goals

To be updated

Objectives

a) Have an established, effective institutional framework for an Early Warning System (EWS) for the East Africa Region b) Strengthen integration between producers and user to develop innovative, accurate tailor-made EWS products through co-production for the East African Region, mainly through activites focused on fellowships and knowledge transfer c) Improve methords and strengthen capacity for communication and promoting understanding and use of EWS products with relevant producers, technicians, forecasters, intermediaries and users in the East African Region

Synopsis

This project addresses soil degradation and its impact on food security and poverty in eastern Africa.

Goals

To scale up soil carbon enhancement interventions with benefit to the rural poor through increased food security and climate adaptation and resilience

Objectives

To help improve policy implementation and programmatic investments focused on scaling up soil carbon enhancement interventions

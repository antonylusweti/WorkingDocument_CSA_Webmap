Synopsis

AgriProFocus brings together farmers, agribusinesses, civil society, knowledge institutes and governments. United in diversity, our members show that agribusiness and development are not mutually exclusive. Together, we find new, sustainable ways of creating impact with business. Exchanging perspectives and expecting the unexpected. Cultivating collaboration through linking, learning and leadership

Goals

AgriProFocus helps you find the right people to solve problems and do business with. Whether you are looking for change makers in the agri-food sector or partners to solve specific challenges with. Our network connects (un)usual suspects, facilitates innovative ways of working together and stimulates long term collaboration

Objectives

a) to facilitates exciting learning environments aimed at innovating your business; b) share best practices, experiences and failures in order to learn from and with each other

Comments

In 2020 AgriProFocus (APF) and the Food & Business Knowledge Platform (F&BKP) are transitioning towards becoming the Netherlands Food Partnership (NFP). NFP’s core mission is to boost transformative approaches for sustainable food systems and healthy diets in Low- and Middle Income Countries. The NFP has been initiated from the Dutch Ministerial level and is to be operational by January 1st 2021.

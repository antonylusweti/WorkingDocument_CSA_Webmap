Synopsis

The project seeks to integrate all various economic sectors among the environment, agriculture, education and forestry for sustainability purposes and welfare of the beneficiaries. It relies on quite a number of issues outlined during a pre-implementation analysis, among them; livelihood, food security, household’s dietary diversity, health, threats to global environmental benefits, threats and stresses on agrobiodiversity, gender differences in household dietary diversity, housing, Water supplies, conservation practices, social situation, vulnerability and land conflicts.

Goals

To help in increasing incomes and improving food security of poor households in the programme intervention areas.

Objectives

To increase the productive physical capital, increasing the resilience of production systems and rural households to climate change, intensify agriculture and animal husbandry including improving the processing and the marketing of products by smallholders and improving nutritional status.

CSA Activities and interventions

Smart water management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops

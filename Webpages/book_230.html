Synopsis

ACCRA is a SADC GIZ programme that supports the implementation of climate-relevant elements of the SADC Regional Agricultural Policy through strengthening the capacity of CCARDESA to act as a knowledge broker, coordinator and moderator for agricultural research and development. The project carries out capacity building for technical staff and managers of different regional, national and local organisations, and it supports closer cooperation between regional and national institutions. At the regional level, it disseminates information about topics related to climate change and agriculture, tailored to the different target groups. At both national and local levels, it is selecting agricultural value chains for which it will identify climate-smart investments.

Synopsis

The CSRP aims are to improve the understanding and prediction of African climate on monthly, seasonal and decadal timescales and to strengthen climate science capacity in Africa.

Goals

The aim was to help identify research directions that will best contribute towards meeting demand in Africa.

Objectives

a) Improve understanding of the drivers of African climate variability and change; b) improve prediction on monthly-to-decadal timescales; c) develop climate monitoring and attribution systems and support use of climate science in Africa.

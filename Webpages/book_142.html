Synopsis

The Mung4-Fe project will develop and promote the cultivation and consumption of mungbean (greengram)genotypes with high concentrations of bio-available iron as means of lessening iron deficiency anemia in the ASALs. 

Goals

To reduce the malnutrition burden among smallholder farming communities in the drought prone areas of East Africa using iron rich mungbean varieties.

Objectives

a) To characterize current mungbean production systems and prevailing constraints; b) To select and characterize advanced mungbean lines for iron content and other nutrients; c) Develop and promote efficient mungbean processing and utilization technologies; d) Strengthen mungbean seed production and delivery systems; e) To develop and promote suitable production and integrated pest management packages and; f) To strengthen the capacity and linkage among mungbean value chain actors.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Postharvesting management and storage

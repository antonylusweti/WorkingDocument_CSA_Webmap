Synopsis

The project aims to increase agricultural productivity, market access and food security in 8 LWH/RSSP sites across the country.

Goals

To increase agricultural productivity, market access, and food security of the targeted beneficiaries in the project areas.

Objectives

a) Institutional Strengthening, Agriculture Productivity Enhancement, and Nutrition Improvement; b) Irrigation and Water Use Efficiency; c) Market Linkages and Value Addition Investment Support

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Smart water management; Markets,credit\finance and value chains for CSA.

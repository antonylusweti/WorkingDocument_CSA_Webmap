Synopsis

Women for Climate Justice is a global network of organisations, experts and activists working for gender equality, women's rights and climate justice.

Goals

The main goal of GenderCC Women for Climate Justice is integrating gender justice into climate change policy at local, national and international levels. GenderCC is working to achieve gender and climate justice

Objectives

a) Raising awareness and building capacity on gender and climate to improve climate policies; b) Increasing the knowledge base on gender and climate to identify effective mitigation and adaptation options; c) Empowering women and men to actively contribute to mitigation and adaptation; d) Enhancing cooperation on gender and climate issues at all level; e) Advocating for gender and climate justice as overarching, guiding principles.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Policy and Advocacy; Postharvest management; Renewable energy for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

Synopsis

The Community-Based Adaptation Network (CBA Network) is a free, online platform aimed at practitioners and organisations working on community-based adaptation to climate change. It is a dynamic space for open discussion about community-based adaptation issues, direct interaction with like minded individuals in our sector, and a place to share resources and case studies.

Goals

The CBA Network aims to be a vibrant community where we’ll develop innovative responses to climate change.

Objectives

a) To create more communication and direct interaction between CBA practitioners. b). To enhance discussion and sharing of ideas linked to CBA. c). To create a space where information regarding CBA can be disseminated to active practitioners quickly. d). To create greater access to an abundant and accessible knowledge base and resource library for the themes of CBA.

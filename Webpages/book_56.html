Synopsis

Vi Agroforestry climate compensation project is located in western Kenya and is called the Kenya Agricultural Carbon Project (KACP).

Goals

The project is aimed at small-scale farmers who have access to less than 1 hectare of land.

Objectives

To help farmers gain knowledge on sustainable agricultural land management (SALM ) and can then implement these on their own farms.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart crops/ promotion of drought tolerant crops; Soil management

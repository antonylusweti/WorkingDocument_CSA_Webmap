Synopsis

The project financed technical assistance, workshops, goods, works, matching grants for associated subprojects, services and operational costs in order to promote sustainable land and water management, agroforestry and shade grown coffee cultivation, as well as conservation activities in one protected area.

Goals

To pilot sustainable land and water management practices in the coffee landscape of Burundi.

Objectives

a) Implementing sustainable land and water management practices in productive areas, in order to prevent further land degradation and rehabilitate degraded lands, b) Establishing a shade grown coffee that promotes environmentally friendly production technologies (polyculture) that includes coffee as well as various types of trees and other plants that provide additional products for income generation and consumption.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Capacity building of local and regional stakeholders; Agroforestry

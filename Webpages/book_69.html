Synopsis

The programme focuses on vocational training and capacity building as a tool for improving agricultural productivity.

Goals

To contribute to an increase in the income of smallholder farmers through professional and vocational training, leading to higher productivity and improved marketing of agricultural products.

Objectives

To support vulnerable groups, with special attention to uneducated youth and young women who are heads of household.

CSA Activities and interventions

Capacity Building; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA

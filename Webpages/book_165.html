Synopsis

Researchers in the Livestock Research Group’s Animal Health & Greenhouse Gas Emissions Intensity Network (AHN) are investigating the connections between animal diseases and GHG emissions intensity. They are also exploring possible GHG mitigation opportunities through disease control.

Synopsis

The project will focus on sustainable livelihoods for people displaced by conflict and those who have returned to their home areas and have access to productive assets such as livestock and microenterprise activities.

Goals

To improve the resilience of households, communities and agriculture systems in stressed and crisis levels of food insecure regions of South Sudan.

Objectives

to increase households’ food and nutrition security and income through increasing production and diversification of livelihoods in a manner which contributes to sustainability and fosters peaceful co-existence.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

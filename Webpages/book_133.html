Synopsis

The objective of the Agricultural Technology and Agribusiness Advisory Services Project (ATAAS) for Uganda is to increase agricultural productivity and incomes of participating households by improving the performance of agricultural research and advisory service systems in the Republic of Uganda. As part of government reforms, the agricultural extension mandate has been transferred from the original agency providing advisory services under the ATAAS project to the Ministry. In addition, the project included technology uptake grants that were intended to support technology demonstrations and multiplication

Goals

To support smallholders to integrate in value chains via provision of agribusiness development services and matching grants to farmer groups and emerging farmers for promising marketing, value addition, and agro?processing activities.

Synopsis

The project is about safeguarding water supply and farming conditions in the Simiyu Region of Tanzania.

Goals

The objective of the Project is to increase the climate resilience of rural and urban households, particularly small scale farmers and women, living in the Simiyu Region and to improve policies and regulation for cross-sectoral action towards climate adaptation. It will contribute to the implementation of the Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs) of Tanzania.

Objectives

a) To improve the government’s institutional and regulatory framework for cross sectoral and community based adaptation planning; b) T improve climate resilience of water supply infrastructure, sanitation services and agricultural practices in the Simiyu Region; c) To enhance adaptive capacities of vulnerable urban and rural population, as well as implementing capacities of local and central government structures

CSA Activities and interventions

Smart water management; Climate smart crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Weather and Agroadvisory services;

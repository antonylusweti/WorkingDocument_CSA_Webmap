Synopsis

Programme brings together existing initiatives and partnerships working in CSA, highlights good practices and success stories, and builds synergies and cooperation among stakeholders to leverage resources towards mutual objectives and minimize duplication of ongoing efforts

Goals

To accelerate the shift towards sustainable food systems. It was developed in the context of the core initiative Setting the table for our children: Improving governance of food systems through multi- stakeholder action.1

Objectives

a) Identify an individual or group of food systems champions; b) Conduct a holistic food systems assessment; c) Initiate a multi-stakeholder process for dialogue and action; e) Strengthen institutional capacity for long term food systems governance

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Policy and Advocacy; Postharvest management; Renewable energy for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

Synopsis

The project is taking various measures to improve their food security and their resilience to crisis situations, ranging from the rehabilitation of pastureland and water points to the introduction of land use plans and the diversification of income sources for women.

Goals

To be updated

Objectives

Improve the food security and resilience of pastoralist communities in the Borana Zone through sustainable and context-specific natural resources management (NRM) practices, along with enhanced income diversification strategies.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Livestock disease and pest management; Smart water management

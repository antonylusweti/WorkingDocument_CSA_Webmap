Synopsis

The Program contributes to reinforce hydro-agricultural infrastructure in the marshland and plains providing access to production areas; develop and organize the rice and dairy value chains; support the diversification of production and improve the nutritional conditions and build institutional capacity in rice and diary value chains.

Goals

To strengthen food security and rural development in the regions of Imbo and Moso.

Objectives

a) reinforce hydro-agricultural infrastructure in the marshland and plains providing access to production areas; b) develop and organize the rice and dairy value chains; c) support the diversification of production and improve the nutritional conditions and d) build institutional capacity among those in the agricultural sector who are engaged in rice and diary value chains.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops, Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops

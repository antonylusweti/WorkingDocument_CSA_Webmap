Synopsis

Climate change is impacting agricultural systems in Kenya. In the future, these impacts are expected to get worse. Thats why this Copernicus demonstrator contract will make climate information available to Kenyan farmers: to support them in adapting to a changing climate.

Goals

To support Kenyan farmers in adapting to a changing climate.

Objectives

To better prepare smallholder farmers in Kenya for a changing climate.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate smart crops/ promotion of drought tolerant crops

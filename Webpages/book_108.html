Synopsis

This project is designed to increase the resilience of women, men and children in Torit State in South Sudan. This will be achieved through improving agricultural production and access to value chains for farmers and small scale businesses.

Goals

To increase the resilience of women, men and children in Torit State

Objectives

a) To improve productivity and reduce water-related diseases; b) To improve access to water; c) To reduce vulnerability to the threat of food & nutrition insecurity

CSA Activities and interventions

Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Capacity Building

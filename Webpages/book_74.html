Synopsis

The Project supports directly two of the five strategic axes of Madagascar’s 2015-2019 National Development Plan (Plan National de Développement [PND]), namely: (i)inclusive growth and territorial anchoring of development and (ii) conserving natural capital and strengthening resilience to risks and catastrophes

Goals

To develop a model for integrated landscape management that can be replicated and scaled up in other parts of Madagascar and thus reach a large number of households.

Objectives

a) To increase access to improved irrigation services and agricultural inputs; b) To strengthen the integrated management of natural resources in the selected landscapes by the local actors; c) To provide immediate and effective response to an eligible crisis or emergency.

CSA Activities and interventions

Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Agroforestry; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds

Synopsis

The aim of project is to reduce the impacts of climate change in agro-pastoral communities of Kongwa District. This will be achieved through implementation of integrated concrete adaptation measures covering the following sectors: water, agriculture and livestock.

Goals

To be updated

Objectives

a) Enhance climate resilient rural water supply system in vulnerable agro-pastoral communities at Mtanana and Ugogoni Wards; b) Support transformation of exploitive agro-pastoral practices to diversified climate smart and sustainable livelihoods; c) Improved ecological functions to sustain climate sensitive livelihoods in Kongwa District; d) Capacities of institutions, extension services and trainers strengthened to reduce risks associated with climate- induced livelihood failures in Kongwa district.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

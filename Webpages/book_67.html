Synopsis

The programme recognizes the critical need for Madagascar to develop and implement strategies to manage climate risk and to mobilize resources to finance priority actions. Programme is guided by 5 principles; Spatial Resilience; Sectoral Resilience; Communities Resilience; Infrastructure Resilience and Financial Resilience.

Goals

To strengthen institutional and technical capacities of the Government of Madagascar to manage and mainstream climate risks into development policies, plans and programmes in key sectors.

Objectives

a) Enhancing the understanding of climate risks and implications; b) Strengthen institutional and policy frameworks for climate resilience

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Capacity Building; Policy

Synopsis

The project gears improving land?based production of the rural poor, while increasing their resilience to climate and disaster risks as well as mitigating land resource?related conflicts. At the community level, the project will involve all its members, it will contribute to mitigating rural inequalities

Goals

To restore land productivity in targeted degraded landscapes and, in the event of an eligible crisis or emergency, to provide immediate and effective response to said eligible crisis or emergency.

Objectives

a) Reduce poverty and achieve shared prosperity in a sustainable manner as constrained by demographic burden and fragility' b) Sustainably manage land and water resources for multiple purposes and functions.

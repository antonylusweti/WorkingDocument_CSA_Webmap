Synopsis

The pilot project sought to increase the resilience of Rwanda’s hydroelectric power sector to support agriculture and water sectors

Goals

To be updated

Objectives

a) Demonstrating village-level activities that contribute to restoration of the Ruguzi–Burera–Ruhundo watershed and increase local resilience to climate change; b) Improving the management of two of Rwanda’s hydroelectric power plants; c) Promoting the integration of climate change considerations into Rwanda’s national energy and sustainable development policies.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Smart water management; Irrigation

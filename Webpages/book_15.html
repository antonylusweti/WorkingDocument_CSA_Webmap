Synopsis

The Fisheries Resources Management Programme (FReMP) intends to invest both in large fisheries and small pelagics.

Goals

To contribute to household food and nutrition security and the alleviation of rural poverty.

Objectives

a) To increase incomes and improved nutrition situation for targeted beneficiaries and sustainable management of natural resources; b) To strengthen production systems and market linkages.

CSA Activities and interventions

Capacity Building; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Smart water management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA

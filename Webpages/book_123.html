Synopsis

The project focuses on the combination of scientific data collection and solution-led field research on climate-smart livestock production. The results are being mainstreamed via large-scale investment projects.

Goals

To increase the contribution of livestock production to the three key pillars of CSA across diverse systems and species in Uganda, Kenya and Ethiopia.

Objectives

a) Improving the reporting of their NDCs in the livestock sector.

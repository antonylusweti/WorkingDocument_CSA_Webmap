Synopsis

The Framework guides the implementation of CSA at national and county government levels. The frameworkis aligned to Kenya's Vision 2030 developmental goals

Goals

To promote climate resilient and low carbon growth sustainable agriculture that ensures food security

Objectives

Create leverage on global opportunities by providing clear structures through which farmers can directly benefit from by tapping the available global climate financing instruments in collaborations with the sector players.

CSA Activities and interventions

All CSA interventions: Agroforestry; Climate Smart Crops; Climate smart livestock practices; Livestock feed management; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Policy and Advocacy; Postharvest management; Renewable energy for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

Synopsis

Project works on hydroagricultural improvement and infrastructure component that will rehabilitate and develop new wetland and watersheds, as well as access roads. Support will also be given for for priority value chains (rice and dairy production)

Goals

To promote increases in incomes and improvements in food security for poor households in the programme area.

Objectives

a) To increase physical productive capital by building the resilience of production systems to climate change; b) To develop and improve crop and livestock production activities and strengthen community-based initiatives, and in turn enhance food security and incomes, c) To intensify crop and livestock farming to improve nutritional status and, ultimately, structure the milk and rice value chains, inter alia, to promote youth employment and strengthen capacity of the actors.

CSA Activities and interventions

Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds

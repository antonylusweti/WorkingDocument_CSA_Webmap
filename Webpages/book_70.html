Synopsis

The project seeks to help strengthen and build smallholder farmers’ resilience to the effects of climate change through the development of irrigated agriculture including spate-irrigation and the natural resource management approaches.

Goals

To improve sustainably the incomes and the food security of smallholder producers of Menabe and Melaky regions in Madagascar

Objectives

To help strengthen and build smallholder farmers resilience to the effects of climate change through the development of irrigated agriculture including spate-irrigation and the natural resource management approaches.

Synopsis

This research project identifies and analyses institutional conditions for scaling low-emission development dairy interventions in an inclusive manner.

Goals

Objectives

To conduct social and biophysical research in conjunction with policy processes and engagements with lead dairy firms aimed at reducing the emissions intensities in the Kenyan and Tanzanian dairy sectors.

Gender & Social Inclusion Information

The project integrates on-going analyses in the CCAFS-project on gendered dimensions of dairy intensification, specifically focusing on how intensification changes womens labour burden, ability to benefit from markets and control of resources under intensification.

Synopsis

The project aims for an inclusive, sustainable and climate-relevant transformation of the Eritrean dairy value chain to enhance food and nutrition security, reduce poverty, create job opportunities for young people, and address other key Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Goals

The project aims to accelerate access to innovation in the Eritrean dairy sector, development and transfer of technologies for primary milk production, and for the more efficient and effective organization of the full value chain.

Objectives

a) Foster innovation in agriculture by generating scientific knowledge and innovations that will encompass agronomy for primary production and within landscapes, technology for all steps of processing, and economy, social affairs and general organisation along the value chains, and focussing on major activities to reduce poverty and vulnerability, support job creation, and food and nutrition security; b) Develop research architecture and enhance national and regional research capacities to be more efficient and conducive to innovation; c) Increase knowledge and evidence to feed development policies and investment decisions.

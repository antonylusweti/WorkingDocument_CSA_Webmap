Synopsis

The proposed programme will strengthen the technical capacity of the targeted countries through regional cooperation to generate and disseminate climate forecasts and projections (at regional and national scales), and the capacity of national organisations to develop, interpret and deliver climate information products that are useful for early action. The regional approach will draw on the collective observation network of IGAD member states to improve the coverage, consistency and availability of regional climate information, using the convening power of IGAD to facilitate technical and climate information sharing mechanisms for increased information and services sharing across the region. The project will also provide technical assistance for mainstreaming climate information into national and regional policies and strategies, with specific focus on the agriculture sector. Finally, early action mechanisms will be established at the regional and national levels, including improved dissemination of early warnings and the development of preparedness and contingency plans for extreme climate events.

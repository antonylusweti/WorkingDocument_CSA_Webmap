Synopsis

This project uses an ex-ante tool that assesses environmental impacts of livestock systems and value chains. It evaluates land requirements, productivity, water use, soil health, economics and greenhouse gas emissions

Goals

Livestock production systems with reduced environmental footprint and increased ecosystem service contributions

Objectives

To contribute towards a pro-poor, environmentally responsible livestock sector investment and development with specific reference to developing and emerging countries

CSA Activities and interventions

Soil Management; Livestock disease and pest managemnet; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Capacity Building

Synopsis

The project aims to increase the productive physical capital, increasing the resilience of production systems and rural households to climate change, intensify agriculture and animal husbandry including improving the processing and the marketing of products by smallholders and improving nutritional status.

Goals

To increase incomes and improve food security of poor households

Objectives

a) To increase the productive physical capital, increasing the resilience of production systems and rural households to climate change; b) Intensify agriculture and animal husbandry including improving the processing and the marketing of products by smallholders; c) To improve nutritional status.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Irrigation schemes and infrastructure; agricultural intensification; Capacity building of local and regional stakeholders

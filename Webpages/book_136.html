Synopsis

The project aimed to develop a viable dairy value chain in two districts of Apac and Kole, which are in the middle north cattle corridor. The two districts are endowed with local resources for dairy development. The focus of the project was on the improvement of livestock feeds and feeding systems among small-scale dairy farmers, and training on improved reproductive technologies and best breeding practices, as well as agribusiness, milk quality and hygiene.

Goals

To improve economic livelihoods in Apac and Kole districts through support for the strategic business development of a dairy value chain.

Objectives

a) Strengthening research, Identifying and building key human resource capacity; technology adaptation at the farm level including modern irrigation technologies; b) Up scaling the transfer and utilization of food-production and labour-saving technologies for women farmers; c) Enhancing extension services; Increasing access to and use of critical farm inputs; d) Promoting sustainable land use and soil management; Increasing access to agricultural finance.

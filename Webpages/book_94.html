Synopsis

The project will target poor and food insecure rural households and includes specific measures to target the poorest people, and women and youth.

Goals

The project's overall goal is to contribute to poverty reduction in the drought-prone Eastern Province of Rwanda.

Objectives

a) improved access to land, forests, water and water bodies for production purposes; b) increased acreage of farmland under water-related infrastructure; c) increased acreage of farmland under climate resilient management and practices; d) increased capacity of smallholder farmers and local government to sustainably manage natural resources and climate-related risks; e) enhanced use by farmers, including youth, of technologies, equipment and infrastructure adapted to smallholder agriculture; and f) increased farmers’ economic benefits from market participation and increased sales.

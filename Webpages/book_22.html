Synopsis

The proposed project aims at improving pastoralist food security and adaptive capacities in the lowlands of Borana Zone, Southern Ethiopia, through enhancing the sustainable management of natural resources.

Goals

Improving pastoralist food security and adaptive capacities in the lowlands of Borana Zone, Southern Ethiopia, through enhancing the sustainable management of natural resources

Objectives

To improve (agro-) pastoralists’ food security and adaptive capacities through improving and strengthening natural resources management practices, governance system and pastoralist income diversification.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Livestock disease and pest management; Smart water management

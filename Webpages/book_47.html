Synopsis

The Global Agenda for Sustainable Livestock (GASL) is a multistakeholder partnership establishedand its main principle is that all livestock production systems can be more sustainable, no matter where they are.

A platform for sharing good practices and policies, and promotes the sustainability of the global livestock sector. I

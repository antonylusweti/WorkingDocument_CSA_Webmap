Synopsis

The East Africa Dairy Development project is a regional industry development program led by Heifer International in partnership with ILRI, TechnoServe, the World Agroforestry Centre (ICRAF) and the African Breeders Service Total Cattle Management.

Goals

To help families living on small 1-5 acre farms lift themselves out of poverty through more profitable production and marketing of milk.

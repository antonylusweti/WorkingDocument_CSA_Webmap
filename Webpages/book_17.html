Synopsis

The NDCs presents tangible actions and procedures that are required to be taken to put into practice the projects and programs. These comprise, capacity building, technology transfer, financial support and building of partnership with regional and international organization/institutions involved in climate change. It is stipulated that for effective implementation of the NDC program, monitoring and evaluation (M & E) tools are important. This includes regular monitoring of the stipulated activities indicated in the project documents to check whether they are on the right track or not towards the targets.The NDC provides a breakdown down action plans for the upcoming 13 to 15 years (2018- 2030), indicated in the INDC by setting three target phases (years) (2020, 2025 and 2030).

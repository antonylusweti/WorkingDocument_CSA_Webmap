Synopsis

AGRA YieldWise intervention seeks to reduce postharvest losses in Tanzania maize value chain by half by 2018 by linking surplus maize producers to input suppliers and output buyers; aggregating and training farmers; improving access to finance; linking entrepreneurs with manufacturers of technologies and supporting prioritization of loss reduction by value chain actors.

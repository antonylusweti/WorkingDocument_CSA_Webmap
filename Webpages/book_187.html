Synopsis

The CGIAR Research Program on Roots, Tubers and Bananas (RTB) is working globally to harness the untapped potential of those crops in order to improve food security, nutrition, income, climate change resilience and gender equity of smallholders.

Goals

RTB together with its partners will achieve impact in the following areas:

Objectives

a) Increasing productivity. b)Supporting diversified nutrisious diets. c)Creating new enterpreneurial and job opportunities. d)Addressing biodiversity loss, soil and landscape degradation and challenges of climate change

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services

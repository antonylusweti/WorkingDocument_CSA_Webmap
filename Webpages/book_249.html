Synopsis

Maisha bora (meaning 'Good life' in Swahili) is a five-year programme (2015-2019) aimed at improving food security in Longido and Simanjiro districts in the north of Tanzania. Fifteen of the most food-insecure villages in these two districts, with a population of mainly pastoralists and agro-pastoralists, have been selected for the programme

Goals

Effective coordination of the programme assuring coherence, induced cooperation and increased implementation capacity.

Objectives

The programme's specific objective is to reach higher and more secured income used for nutrition, and improved local availability of food for 9,000 households in 15 villages in Simanjiro and Longido districts, and in particular for 40% of impoverished households, women and youth

Synopsis

The project is national in scope targeting poor and food-insecure households with particular attention to households headed by women, which will receive priority in land allocation in new irrigation zones.

Goals

To sustainably raise smallholder farmers' productivity through improved water resources and integrated agricultural production.

Objectives

a) Development of a national seed system and research to develop improved crop varieties and produce breeder and foundation seeds; b) Support to three seed-processing centres and enhancement of seed certification services; c) Intensification of irrigated and rainfed crop production through the provision of fertilizers, agrochemicals, improved seeds, conservation agriculture trials and improved extension services; d) Promotion of small livestock investments for the lowest-income earners and women with limited access to land.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Capacity Building; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Smart water management

Synopsis

The African Research and Impact Network brings together a network of scholars across Africa and who have been undertaking research in various fields including natural resource management, climate change, agriculture, forestry, energy, water and cities to leverage their knowledge and experiences in promoting research excellence and impact pathways. The team includes talented researchers and technocrats expected to pursue a common goal.

Goals

ARIN’s core focus is on peer learning and sharing good transformative research and impact practices across Africa. Modelled as a network, ARIN seeks to leverage on the capabilities of African talented scholars in a flexible manner.

Objectives

a) Network members have the opportunity to engage in periodic intellectual discussions on the future of Africa’s research and impact pathways. b) Members also engage in development and implementation grant projects.

Synopsis

These programme supports the launch of key needed reforms on agriculture, land-use planning and related national land use scheme, energy, family planning, forests and a tenure policy to better secure tenure in the rural sector.

Goals

To be updated

Objectives

a) Capacity building of government services and local communities for the management of the Territories; b) Development of agriculture and livestock in the savannah, c) Improvement of cultivation techniques in forests and savannahs; d) Support for the most vulnerable such as Indigenous Peoples as identified in the Indigenous Peoples Development Plans (PDPA)

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Capacity building of local and regional stakeholders

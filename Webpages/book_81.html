Synopsis

The project is implemented in Djibouti, Kenya, Sudan and Uganda in IGAD regions considered the most vulnerable and prone to drought

Goals

To increase the resilience of smallholder farmers and pastoralists to climate change risks, through the establishment of appropriate early warning systems and the implementation of drought adaptation actions

Objectives

a) Promote investments in drought early warning systems (EWS); b) Strengthen and improve the capacity of key stakeholders in drought risks management; c) Support communities to undertake innovative adaptation actions that reinforce their resilience to drought; d) Enhance knowledge management and information sharing on drought resilience

CSA Activities and interventions

Smart Water management; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops

Synopsis

A4NH is built on the notion that agriculture can do much more than reduce hunger and poverty it has an enormous potential to significantly improve the nutrition and health of people around the world.

Goals

A4NH seeks to realize the enormous potential of agricultural development to make significant contributions to improving the nutrition and health of people worldwide.

Objectives

A4NH is designed to fill the existing gap between agricultural development and its unfulfilled health and nutritional benefits

Water Security and Climate Resilience Project in Kenya

3 Keywords

Start Year

Status

Ongoing

Synopsis

Increase availability and productivity of irrigation water and enhance the institutional framework and strengthen capacity for water security and climate resilience

Goals

The overall objective of this component will be to strengthen the enabling institutional and legal framework to sustainably advance Kenya's vast water sector investment program in order to achieve water security and climate resilience.

Objectives

a) Increase availability and productivity of irrigation water for project beneficiaries; b) Enhance the institutional framework and strengthen capacity for water security and climate resilience for the country.

CSA Activities and interventions

Capacity building; Climate Smart crops/promotion of drought tolerant crops; Markets, credit/finance and valuee chains for CSA

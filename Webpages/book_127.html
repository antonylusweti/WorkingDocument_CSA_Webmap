Synopsis

Project aims to integrate weather and climate information into national policies, annual work plans and local development including the National Policy for Disaster Preparedness and Management, and district and sub-county development plans in the priority districts of Bukedi, Busoga, Elgon, Teso, Acholi, Karamoja and Lango sub-regions.

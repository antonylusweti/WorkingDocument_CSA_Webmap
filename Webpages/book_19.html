Synopsis

The project aims to build soil and water conservation structures on heavily eroded land and gullies created by floods and water run offs - such as dry masonry check dams, gabion, soil bunds, stone bands, and terraces.

Goals

To improve food security and livelihood of poor and vulnerable people

Objectives

To build soil and water conservation structures on heavily eroded land and gullies created by floods and water run offs - such as dry masonry check dams, gabion, soil bunds, stone bands, and terraces.

Synopsis

A dynamic and inclusive platform for exchanging information on climate-smart agriculture (CSA) activities in Kenya.

Goals

The principal aim of the MSP is to create a framework that facilitates the dissemination, coordination and sharing of CSA programmatic planning and implementation, as well as receiving and incorporating feedback from participating stakeholders. T

Objectives

a) Support the profiling of CSA actions and interventions in the country. b) Strengthen capacities of institutions in planning, implementation, monitoring and evaluation as well as reporting of CSA across stakeholders at all levels. c) Identify opportunities for investment in CSA by platform members and others. d) Support the gathering of data to build an evidence base for decision making and policy development.

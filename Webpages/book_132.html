Synopsis

The project aims to reduce Uganda's agricultre sector's vulnerability to impacts of Climate Change by disseminating practical knowledge on climate-reslient agricultural proctices at a community-level, through Farmer Field Schools.

Goals

The project also seeks to mainsteam Climate Change Adaptation into Uganda's agriculture sector's policies and plans.

Objectives

To build climate resilience into the agricultural sector, as an effective means of reducing vulnerability and disseminating community-level adaptation measures.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Capacity Building

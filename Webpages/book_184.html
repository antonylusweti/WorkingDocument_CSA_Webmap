Synopsis

WISER aims at helping people across Africa -focusing initially on East Africa (Ethiopia, Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda and Burundi) to be more resilient to natural disasters and climate change by 2030.

Goals

To enhance the resilience of poor people and of economic development in Africa through improved generation and use of weather and climate information services.

Objectives

1)To increase the use of improved, reliable, co-produced and accessible weather and climate services to inform regional, national, sub-national and community level policy, planning and decision-making in Africa. 2)To ensure improved access to weather and climate information at regional, national, sub-national and community levels changed through strengthened capacity of and integration between NMHSs collaborators and users that promotes improved service development and delivery.

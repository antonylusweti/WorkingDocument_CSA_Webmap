Synopsis

The goal of the Agriculture and Food Security program is to support innovation for more efficient and sustainable agricultural production to enhance food security, incomes, and nutrition that benefit small-scale farmers (particularly women).

Goals

To be updated

Objectives

a) Focus on new or underutilized crops, fruits, and vegetables, and high-value animal products to boost both incomes and nutrition. b) Support projects with high potential for transferability and adoption with the active participation of the private sector or social entrepreneurship organizations. C) Contribute to the development of improved market access and agricultural value chains for smallholders. d) Support programming on financial services, small-scale mechanization, reduction of post-harvest losses, and youth employment in the agricultural sector.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Policy and Advocacy; Postharvest management; Renewable energy for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building; Tools and Methodologies; Insurance

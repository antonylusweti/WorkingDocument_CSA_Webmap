Synopsis

The project was developed to address three intertwined challenges; ensuring food security through increased productivity and income, adapting to climate change and contributing to climate change mitigation.

Goals

To increase the number of farmers using Climate Smart Agricultural practises

Objectives

a) TO increase the number of farmers using climate smart agricultural practises; b) Establish Climate Smart gardens; c) Improve agricultural inputs supply and develop climate smart agricultural enterprises; d) Develop a monitoring and evaluation system for Climate Smart Agricultural technologies.

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; Markets, credit/finance and value chains for CSA; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building;

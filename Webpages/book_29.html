Synopsis

The project combines improved water access and resource management in line with the Ethiopian Water Resources Management Policy to enable the most vulnerable communities to adapt to shocks of climate variability in the future.

Goals

To improve health, food and water security as well as strengthen institutional and regulatory systems for gender and climate- responsive planning and development

Objectives

a) Introducing solar-powered water pumping and small-scale irrigation; b) Rehabilitation and management of degraded lands around the water sources; c) creating an enabling environment by raising awareness and improving local capacity.

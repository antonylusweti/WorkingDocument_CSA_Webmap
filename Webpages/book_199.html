Synopsis

Project was a research / normative initiative aimed at generating two major public goods, including a survey tool targeting livestock keeping households and effective methodologies to provide policy assistance.

Goals

The project supported capacity building and facilitates national-level processes for evidence-based policy reforms in the livestock sector in Tanzania and Uganda. It generated a number of public goods, anticipated to improve the way livestock data and statistics are collected, disseminated, analyzed and used by national and international stakeholders.

Objectives

Identify and document effective practices to collect livestock data at household level; Identify and document effective practices to facilitate the dissemination and use of livestock data; Draw lessons and make recommendations to improve the information content of major livestock-related policy documents at country level; Participate in, support and document processes aimed at promoting evidence-based policy reforms in the livestock sector.

Title

Strengthening Climate Information and Early Warning Systems for Climate Resilient Development and Adaptation to Climate Change in Uganda

3 Keywords

Early Warning Systems; Climate Information

Start Year

2012

End Year

2016

Status

Completed

Synopsis

The project aims to strengthen the weather, climate and hydrological monitoring capabilities, early warning systems and available information for responding to extreme weather and planning adaptation to climate change in Uganda.

Goals

To be updated

Objectives

a) Establish a functional network of meteorological and hydrological monitoring stations and associated infrastructure to better understand climatic changes; b) Increase adaptive capacity to respond to the impacts of climate change, including variability, at local, national, regional and global level; c) Promote transfer and adoption of adaptation technology

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Crop Disease and pest management; Livestock disease and pest management; A; Smart water management; Soil management; Weather & Agroadvisory Services; Capacity Building;

Synopsis

Fourth Transformation of Agriculture Sector Program and Second Phase of Program-for-Results Projectis an articulation of how the government proposes to implement the National Agricultural Policy (NAP). PSTA4 is also aligned with National Policies and Strategies including Vision 2050, Rwanda’s National Strategy for Transformation (NST 1), and relevant sub-sector strategies.

Goals

To promote the commercialization of agriculture value chains in Rwanda.

Objectives

To improve the capacity of selected public agricultural institutions and to enable an increase in commercialization of agriculture value chains in Rwanda

CSA Activities and interventions

Climate Smart Crops/Promotion of drought tolerant crops; Climate smart livestock practices/Livestock feed management/Improved breeds; Markets,credit\finance and value chains for CSA.
